# AI Culture Lab Bible

## Vision

Exploring AI-native interaction experiences for cultural learning, storytelling, and heritage.

## Audience

- ABC children
- Cultural enthusiasts

## Principles

- Culture should be experienced, not only explained.
- AI should enhance curiosity.
- Interaction comes before information.
- Avoid stereotypical cultural expressions.

## Constraints

- Solo developer
- Monthly prototype cycle
- Low cost
- Portfolio focused
